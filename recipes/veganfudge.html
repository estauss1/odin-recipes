Vegan Fudge

Vegan fudge made with few ingredients

A vegan fudge recipe that everyone will love and is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces of vegan semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil

Steps

  1. Grease an 8 inch square glass baking dish with coconut oil.
  2. combine chocolate chips, sweetened condensed coconut milk, and 1/4 cup coconut oil in a saucepan. Let sit over low heat, stirring only as needed until chips are melted, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish. Refrigerate until fudge is firm, 3 to 4 hours, or put in freezer for a faster setting time.
